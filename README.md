# Hyperlocal-Urban-Flood-Risk-Predictive-Mapper
This project will help us to predict the risk of local urban flooding in nearby areas by using the topography, geography and historical data  of the neighbourhood of the area.
<br>
We will use data of the local area which shall include the topography of this area , the historical dtaa regarding rainfall patterns and flooding , and also the recent weather conditions such as the temperature ,humidity and precipitation .
